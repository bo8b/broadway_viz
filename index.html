Financial Metrics for Historic Broadway Shows

Bob Manasco, manasco2@illinois.edu

22 July 2020

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has caused an unprecedented hiatus for Broadway theatrical shows in New York City. On March 12, 2020, all Broadway theatres closed their doors as mandated by New York Governor Andrew Cuomo and (as of this writing) have remained closed ever since.

In this interactive slideshow, we will examine the history of Broadway shows as far back as 1901, we will look at attendance trends over the past 25 years, and we will delve into the financial metrics of this show business.
